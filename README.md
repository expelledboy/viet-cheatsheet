# 🇻🇳 Vietnamese Cheat Sheet

A comprehensive, mobile-first Vietnamese language reference — built as a single HTML file, hosted for free, and ready to use in seconds.

**[Open the cheat sheet →](https://expelledboy.github.io/viet-cheatsheet/)**

Whether you're a first-time traveler trying to order coffee in Hội An, or a beginner drilling tones on your phone, this is the reference you'll actually reach for. No app to install, no account to create — just open it and go.

---

## Two modes, one file

**Reference mode** — everything is visible at once, organized into collapsible sections. Scan the page and find what you need fast.

**Learning mode** — cards flip to hide the Vietnamese (or the English). Tap to reveal. Self-quiz on the go without any extra tools.

Switch between them with a single toggle. Your preference is saved.

---

## What's inside

25 sections covering the full beginner-to-conversational arc:

- **Foundations** — tones, vowels, pronunciation rules, Telex typing input
- **Grammar** — pronouns, sentence structure, time & aspect particles, classifiers
- **Numbers** — counting, money, telling time
- **Conversation** — introductions, pleasantries, social language, survival phrases
- **Real-world vocabulary** — café ordering, getting around, common verbs
- **Culture** — reactions & slang, example sentences, etiquette tips
- **Study guide & word bank** — where to go next and key vocab to memorize

---

## Why it's built this way

- **Zero dependencies** — one `.html` file. No frameworks, no CDNs, no build step.
- **Mobile-first** — designed for a phone screen, scales naturally to desktop.
- **Dark & light mode** — follows your system preference and remembers your choice.
- **Instant access** — hosted on GitHub Pages; bookmark it and it's always there.
- **Collapsible sections** — collapse what you know, focus on what you don't.
- **Tap to pronounce** — 🔊 buttons on Vietnamese words and phrases use your device's built-in speech engine to read them aloud. No audio files required.

---

## Running locally

```sh
git clone https://github.com/expelledboy/viet-cheatsheet.git
open viet-cheatsheet/index.html
```

That's it.
